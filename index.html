About AP Janmabhoomi

The Government of Andhra Pradesh, alongside The Special Representative for North America endeavors to bridge NRIs to the native land. AP JANMABHOOMI, a social initiative to give back to the motherland, comprises of three schemes- Digital Classrooms, Anganwadi Centers and Crematoriums. With no Government who has ever taken such a dynamism, the Andhra Pradesh Government provides this platform by providing 70% of the total cost. The rest 30% is funded by NRIs who want to help the motherland. This platform intents on working for the advancement of the Sunrise state. Digital Classrooms establish a stage to match up global levels of education. Anganwadi Centers promote preschool education, healthcare and hygiene for children and pregnant women. Crematoriums ensures the last rites are paid right. The Andhra Pradesh government and the Special Representative for North America not only facilitates the progress of the projects but also assures that each donation is executed. So come along, be a part of many more firsts, support us in our dream to make our Janmabhoomi a superior place to live

DIGITAL CLASS ROOMS

ANGANWADI CENTRES

AP Janmabhoomi is working to restructure traditional Anganwadi centers by equipping them with digital aids. The project is scaling their reach to ensure maternal healthcare and early childhood care and development of children. The project focuses on improving nutrition & healthcare among pre-school going kids and lactating and pregnant women.


FUND A CREMATORIUM

The lack of well-kept crematoriums in many parts of India eludes many of the right to carry out rites for their deceased kin. The Government of Andhra Pradesh is focusing on setting up crematoriums or Mahaprasthanams to help bereaved families conduct the last rites for the deceased with respect and dignity.
